Trotter is a discord bot for I created for some simple moderation. It also uses [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/), 
[HowLongToBeat](https://howlongtobeat.com/), and [IsThereAnyDeal](https://isthereanydeal.com/) to provide the 
following useful commands:  

<br/>

* **Check your playtime for a specific game**
* **Check completion time for a game**
* **Find the best deal for a game**
